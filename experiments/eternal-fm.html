Eternal FM

An infinite-playlist machine. Every song is decoded once, then played beat‑by‑beat — so a “quantum leap” to a musically similar moment is just a different read offset, completely gapless. A from‑scratch DSP pass (FFT → onsets → beat grid → chroma + timbre) decides where it can jump. Press play and let it run.
